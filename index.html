Idea, innovation, vision and challenges in New Bangladesh

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation integrates advanced technologies like AI, cloud computing, and IoT to enhance business efficiency and innovation.

Green Energy

Green energy uses renewable resources like solar, wind, and hydroelectric power to reduce environmental impact.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure incorporates smart technologies, sustainable materials, and efficient systems to improve urban living and transportation.

"Bangladesh 2.0 is not just about economic growth; it’s about creating a society that works for everyone. Through technology and social entrepreneurship, we can empower the poorest, giving them the tools to change their lives. By harnessing the power of innovation, we can solve some of the world’s most pressing challenges. The potential of this country lies in the hands of its people, especially the youth. We must encourage them to think creatively and act boldly. If we nurture their ideas, Bangladesh will emerge as a model of sustainable development. Together, we can build a future where no one is left behind."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
